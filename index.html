List of Games I'm Currently Playing



Brief Descriptions of the Games


Neverwinter is a MMO-Action RPG. It is free‑to‑play on PC, Xbox One and PS4. The campaign does a good job of teaching the gameplay elements as you progress making it easy to pick up and play for the casual gamer but it is robust enough for the hardcore gamer as well. The end game elements will require some serious grinding to acquire and upgrde the end game weapons and armor. If you are into the social aspects there are guilds you can create or join, alliances betweem guilds, guild strongholds to upgrade for better markets and boons and pvp if you enjoy taking on other players.

Final Fantasy XV is the newest of the Final Fantasy series and is visually amazing. The gameplay is fun and easy to pick up on, but the story is what its really about. This game has indepth character development and plot that always seems like your playing a blockbuster movie. If you played the others then you need to check this one out as well.

Magic Duels is a free collectible strategy card game for PC, Xbox and mobile devices. Based on the original CCG developed in 1993. If you enjoy these types of games then this is one you shouldn't miss.
